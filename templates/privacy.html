Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, government-issued IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. Specifically, we use it to display your expense history, generate summaries and category breakdowns, and maintain your account.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the expense tracking service. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers with reasonable security measures in place. While we take precautions to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove your data from our systems. This action cannot be undone.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface. When your browser loads these fonts, Google's servers may log your IP address in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. No financial or personal data from your account is shared with Google or any other third party.

We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-broker services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may do this directly within the app or by contacting us. We will respond to any data-related requests within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app. We take privacy seriously and will do our best to address your concerns promptly.
